Security Operation Centers (SOC)

A Security Operation Center (SOC) is a central function within an organization that uses people, processes, and technology to continuously monitor and improve an organization's security posture while preventing, detecting, analyzing, and responding to cybersecurity incidents. We supply, install and commission SOC rooms based on our clients' networks and requirements.
