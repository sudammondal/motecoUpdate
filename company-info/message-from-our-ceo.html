Message from our President & CEO

Eng. Farhan Bin Naif Al-Faisal
President & CEO

Our defense and security communications, operations and maintenance activities have grown significantly since 2015. Since then, our vision, ambition, enthusiasm and perseverance have driven us to grow and expand.

Our goal was to create the largest telecom and information technology system integrator in Saudi Arabia. And we have succeeded in doing so.

In our continued pursuit of growth and development, 2021 is a milestone year in which we focus on unique and customized solutions.

Through our commitment to solid values and our pursuit of excellence, we have created a brand known for high-quality services, respect for customers and business partners, prompt deliveries, efficiency in operating costs, creativity and an environment of open communication.

We intend to continue building on this foundation. We have set new goals and are taking on new challenges. We look forward to new horizons with a development strategy based on three fundamental principles:

  • - Further expansion of our international business activities
  • - Continuous development of our outstanding employees
  • - Leveraging our strategic alliances with leading defense and security companies

By focusing in this direction, we will be able to play a critical role in Vision 2030 and play an important role in the development of Saudi ICT.
